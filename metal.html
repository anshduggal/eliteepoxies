Balance Seal™ Metal Filled Epoxy

Product Overview

Balance Seal™ Metal Filled Epoxy Compound is a specially formulated high-strength epoxy system filled with metallic powders such as iron, brass, copper, aluminium, and steel to provide enhanced mechanical strength, wear resistance, and improved thermal performance. The compound is designed for industrial repair, rebuilding, sealing, and metal restoration applications where strong bonding and durable metallic reinforcement are required.

Key Features

  • Metal-filled epoxy formulation containing iron, brass, copper, aluminium, and steel fillers
  • Excellent adhesion to ferrous and non-ferrous metals
  • High compressive strength and superior wear resistance
  • Good thermal conductivity and dimensional stability
  • Suitable for rebuilding worn metal surfaces and structural repairs
  • Machinable after full curing (drilling, grinding, and machining possible)

Applications

  • Repair and rebuilding of worn metal components
  • Industrial maintenance and restoration of machinery parts
  • Filling cracks, cavities, and surface defects in metal structures
  • Structural bonding and reinforcement applications
  • Engineering repair works requiring metallic strength and durability

Application Note

Mix the components thoroughly in the recommended ratio until a uniform compound is obtained. Apply immediately on clean, dry, and grease-free surfaces for best performance.
