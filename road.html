Balance Seal™ Special Epoxy for Road Furniture Fixing

Product Overview

Balance Seal™ Special Epoxy Compound is a heavy-duty three-component epoxy anchoring and fixing system specially designed for the installation of road safety equipment such as reflectors, road studs, sign posts, barriers, and other structural safety fixtures. The formulation provides excellent adhesion to concrete, asphalt, stone, and metal surfaces, delivering superior bonding strength, long-term durability, and high resistance to weathering, vibration, and mechanical impact.

Key Features

  • Three-component epoxy system for heavy-duty fixing applications
  • Excellent adhesion to concrete, asphalt, stone, and metal surfaces
  • High mechanical strength and impact resistance
  • Durable performance under traffic load and environmental exposure
  • Low shrinkage and reliable long-term structural stability

Pack Composition

  • Part A – Resin: 1 kg
  • Part B – Hardener: 500 g
  • Part C – Filler: 3 kg

Application Instructions

  1. Pour Part A (Resin) and Part B (Hardener) into an open mixing container.
  2. Use a mechanical mixer to mix Part A and Part B thoroughly until a uniform mixture is obtained.
  3. Gradually add Part C (Filler) while mixing continuously until a homogeneous compound is formed.
  4. Apply the prepared compound immediately for fixing road safety equipment in the required position.
