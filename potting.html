Balance Seal™ Potting Compound

Product Overview

Balance Seal™ Epoxy Potting Compound is a high-performance, two-component filled epoxy system designed for reliable sealing, insulation, and encapsulation of electronic assemblies and sensitive components. Engineered for durability and long-term protection, this compound provides excellent mechanical strength, chemical resistance, and environmental protection for a wide range of electrical and electronic applications.

Key Features

  • Two-component filled epoxy system (Resin + Hardener)
  • Excellent sealing and encapsulation performance
  • Superior electrical insulation properties
  • Strong adhesion to metals, plastics, and electronic substrates
  • Good resistance to moisture, vibration, and environmental stress
  • Fully machinable after curing (drilling, grinding, and machining possible)

Applications

  • Potting and encapsulation of electronic circuits
  • Protection of sensors, transformers, and control modules
  • Electrical component sealing and insulation
  • Industrial electronic assemblies and gadgets
  • Protection of PCB assemblies against moisture, dust, and vibration

Technical Information

  • Mixing Ratio: 10 : 1 (100 parts Resin : 10 parts Hardener)
  • Initial Setting Time: 4 – 6 hours (at room temperature)
  • Full Cure Time: Approximately 16 hours (at room temperature)
  • Post-Cure Properties: Fully cured material can be drilled, ground, or machined as required
  • Pack Size: Net Weight 1 kg

Application Guidelines

Ensure surfaces are clean, dry, and free from oil, grease, or dust.

Mix resin and hardener thoroughly in the specified ratio (10:1) until a uniform mixture is obtained.

Pour or apply the mixed compound into the required area for sealing or encapsulation.

Allow to set undisturbed for the recommended curing time to achieve optimal performance.

Storage & Handling

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep containers tightly closed when not in use. Follow recommended safety precautions while handling epoxy materials.
