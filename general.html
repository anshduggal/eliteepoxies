Balance Seal™ General Purpose Epoxy

Product Overview

Balance Seal™ General Purpose Epoxy Compound is a high-performance two-component epoxy system consisting of BS06 Resin and BS-53 Hardener. It is designed for strong bonding, sealing, potting, and general industrial fixing applications. The formulation provides excellent adhesion, superior mechanical strength, and reliable long-term performance across a wide range of industrial materials including metals, glass, ceramics, stone, plastics, and composite substrates. Once cured, the compound delivers durable structural integrity along with excellent electrical insulation characteristics, making it suitable for both mechanical and electrical engineering applications.

Key Features

  • Two-component epoxy system (Resin BS06 + Hardener BS-53)
  • Excellent adhesion to multiple industrial materials
  • High strength, toughness, and long-term durability
  • Outstanding electrical insulating properties
  • Negligible shrinkage after curing for dimensional stability
  • Reliable sealing, bonding, and encapsulation performance

Applications

  1. Electrical and electronic component sealing and insulation
  2. Industrial bonding, repairs, and maintenance work
  3. Glass fixing and installation applications
  4. Marble, granite, and stone fixing
  5. Potting and encapsulation of industrial assemblies
  6. General-purpose industrial structural bonding

Industrial Use Statement: The contents of this pack have been specially prepared for exclusive industrial use as a raw material or for servicing industrial requirements in electronic, electrical engineering, glass, stone fixing, and related industrial applications.
